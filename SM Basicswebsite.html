Stock Market Basics

The stock market is a place where shares of companies are bought and sold. When you buy a share, you become a small owner of that company. Prices go up and down based on company performance, news, and investor demand. Understanding basics helps beginners invest with confidence.

Fundamental Analysis

Fundamental analysis studies a company’s financial health. It includes checking revenue, profit, balance sheet, debt, and future growth. Investors use this method to find strong companies for long-term investment.

Technical Analysis

Technical analysis focuses on price charts and patterns. It uses indicators like moving averages, RSI, and support–resistance levels. This method helps traders decide when to buy or sell stocks in the short term.

Risk Management

Risk management is about protecting your money. It includes using stop-loss, investing only what you can afford to lose, and diversifying your portfolio. Good risk.
